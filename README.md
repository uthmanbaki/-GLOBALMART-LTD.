# -GLOBALMART-LTD.
# Executive Summary

This report presents an analysis of GlobalMart Ltd.'s supply chain and business performance using Power BI. The project involved cleaning raw operational data in Power Query, creating DAX measures, and developing two interactive dashboards to evaluate operational efficiency and business performance. The analysis focuses on revenue generation, supplier performance, shipping efficiency, profitability, and product category performance to support data-driven decision-making.


# BUSINESS PROBLEM STATEMENT
1. Inefficient Delivery Performance: 

The Supply Chain Dashboard shows an average shipping duration of 10 days, indicating that delivery efficiency can still be improved. Supplier performance varies significantly, with some suppliers contributing substantially more orders and revenue than others, suggesting possible inconsistencies in delivery performance.

2. Inventory Imbalance: 

The dashboard indicates varying order volumes across product categories. High-demand categories require continuous monitoring to ensure inventory levels match customer demand and avoid stock shortages or excess inventory.

3. Stagnating Profit Margins: 

Although the company generated approximately $712.55 million in revenue and $195.67 million in profit, profit growth is lower than revenue growth, suggesting that operational costs and supplier pricing may be affecting profitability.

4. Limited Data Visibility: 

The newly developed Power BI dashboards provide centralized, interactive reporting with filters for Region, Supplier, Product Category, and Order Date, significantly improving management's ability to monitor business performance.



#  PROJECT OBJECTIVES

The project successfully addressed the stated objectives by:

Identifying supplier performance across the supply chain.

Key Business Questions & Answers

Which suppliers are underperforming?

Based on supplier performance, smaller suppliers such as Fasttrack, PrimeLog, QuickShip Unknown, and AfroParts contribute significantly less revenue than the leading suppliers, indicating comparatively weaker performance.

Which shipping mode is most efficient?

The dashboard shows Standard Shipping with the shortest average shipping duration, while Overnight (O/N) shipping records the longest shipping duration, indicating lower efficiency.

Are inventory levels aligned with demand?

The dashboard does not include a Stock Level versus Order Quantity visualization; therefore, inventory alignment cannot be conclusively determined from the available dashboard.

Which product categories experience the highest damage rates?

Damaged goods are fairly evenly distributed across product categories, with no single category showing a disproportionately high damage rate.
